This is an app that caches your favorite parts of the Web to your computer, so you and your colleagues can do webmaking while offline or on a bandwidth-constrained internet connection.

To access your cached network over wi-fi, point other browsers to one of the following URLs:

To use it, follow these instructions:

  1. When online, run this app on your computer and browse or spider the sites you want to be able to access and/or remix later.

  2. Go offline! You and your colleagues should be able to browse all the sites you visited/spidered earlier. You can even activate the Web X-Ray Goggles to remix them and save your remixes.

If you're having trouble with connectivity, you may want to use the Diagnostics page.
